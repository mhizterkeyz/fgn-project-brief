Project Brief

What we are setting out to achieve

Project Overview

This application helps to track/monitor government funds down to how they are disbursed and spent for the people of the country to see for themselves and hopefully evaluate the efficiency of government and reduce corruption.

Like a wise man once said: "Shine the torch light, and reduce corruption"

Features

  • Explore budgets
  • Explore funds
  • Graphical data
  • Tweet-bot that posts summary data to twitter
  • Ability to fetch data from other sites by web crawling
  • Giving a voice to the masses by comment sections
  • Money/funds tracking to the roots

Target audience

The major audience is the masses

The app has to be accessible to everyone for free and without any form of prerequisite signup or login.

Users should be able to use app as a source of truth when it comes to matters of tracking the financial expenses of the government

Platform

This will mainly be a web application.

The above means that our application should be able to run on anything that has a browser and an internet connection
